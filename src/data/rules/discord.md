## Discord Rules
1. This Discord is a neutral place
> Racist, homophobic, sexist, and/or religious comments are prohibited
2. Harassment, threats, or personal attacks against any member are not allowed
3. Links to fraudulent, pornographic, illegal, or hacking-related websites are prohibited.
4. Discussion or promotion of illegal activities is forbidden.
5. Spamming or flooding is not allowed
> This applies to both text and images.
6. Advertising or self-promotion without staff permission is prohibited.
7. Do not bypass filters, punishments, or moderation actions.
8. Respect staff decisions
> Arguing with staff members is not allowed. If you see a staff member abusing powers, open
> a [ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1426898495230709852/1426918443533471744).
9. Use channels for their intended purpose.
10. Follow [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms)
    and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines) at all times.

> a [ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1426898495230709852/1426918443533471744).
